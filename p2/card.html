Fennec fox

Its name comes from the Arabic word فنك (fanak), which means fox, and the species name zerda comes from the Greek word xeros which means dry, referring to the fox's habitat.

  • Kingdom: North Africa and Asia
  • Class: Mammalia
  • Family: Canidae
  • Behavior: make a variety of sounds, including barking, a purring sound similar to that of a domestic cat

The coat is often a cream color and fluffy, which deflects heat during the day and keeps the fox warm at night. The fennec's characteristic ears are the largest among all foxes relative to body size, and serve to dissipate heat, as they have many blood vessels close to the skin.
